Step 1 — Product, Niche & Business Model

सबसे पहले तय करें कि आप क्या बेचेंगे और किसे बेचेंगे। Generic store चलाने के बजाय niche select करें — niche में competition कम और audience specific होने की वजह से conversion बेहतर होता है.

Popular business models

  • D2C (Direct to Consumer): अपना brand बनाकर सीधे customers को बेचें.
  • B2C / B2B: Consumers या Businesses को target करें.
  • Dropshipping: Inventory nahi rakhe—supplier directly ship karega.
  • Print on Demand: Customized products bina stock ke.

Product validation (quick tests)

  • Google Trends se demand check karein.
  • Social media groups / Telegram channels me audience ka response dekhen.
  • Small paid campaigns (₹500‑₹1000) run karke product interest test karein.

Step 2 — Domain, Branding & Legal Basics

Domain: Short, memorable aur brandable naam choose karein. .com ya .in dono fine hain. Domain availability check karne ke liye GoDaddy/Namecheap/Hostinger use karein.

Branding: Ek simple logo, color palette aur typography decide karein. Canva ya Figma se design prototypes banayein.

Legal basics: GST registration (India), Terms of Service, Privacy Policy aur Returns policy site par zaroor dalein.

Comments

Step 3 — Platform Comparison & Recommendation (2025)

Platform choice aapke budget, technical skill aur growth plan par depend karta hai. 2025 me kuch important options:

1. Shopify

Managed, fast setup, best for non‑technical founders; monthly fees apply.

2. WooCommerce (WordPress)

Flexible, cost‑effective, self‑hosted; best for control and SEO.

3. Wix / Squarespace

Beginner-friendly, limited scalability.

4. Custom Build (React/Next.js + Node/Laravel)

Full control, scalable, good for unique UX — recommended when you have long-term plans and budget for development and maintenance.

Comments

Step 4 — Hosting, SSL & Performance

For WooCommerce: choose managed WordPress hosting (Cloudways, SiteGround, Hostinger). For custom apps: cloud VPS (DigitalOcean, AWS Lightsail) or managed cloud (AWS/GCP/Azure).

Must-haves

  • SSL certificate (Let's Encrypt free)
  • CDN (Cloudflare)
  • Image optimization — use WebP
  • Caching — server-side + plugin level

Comments

Step 5 — Design & UX Best Practices

Design ka goal conversions aur trust build karna hona chahiye. Mobile-first soch kar design karein kyunki India me zyada users mobile se aate hain.

Checklist

  • Clean hero section with clear CTA
  • Easy navigation & categories
  • Fast product search & filters
  • Trusted review section
  • One-page checkout for better conversions

Comments

Step 6 — Product Pages & SEO

Product pages should be rich, informative and optimized for search engines:

On‑page essentials

  • Unique title and meta description
  • High-quality images + alt texts
  • Structured data (Product schema)
  • Long description with keywords and benefits
  • Customer reviews & FAQ section on product page

Comments

Step 7 — Payments, Security & Trust Signals

Payment options in India: Razorpay, PayU, Cashfree, Instamojo; Global: Stripe & PayPal. Integrate card payments, UPI & wallets to reduce dropoffs.

Comments

Step 8 — Shipping, Tax & Legal Pages

Decide shipping strategy: fixed, real-time courier rates, free shipping above threshold. For India, consider logistics partners like Shiprocket, Delhivery, Ecom Express.

Comments

Step 9 — Launch Checklist

  • Test checkout flow (orders + payments)
  • Test on multiple devices and browsers
  • Set up Google Analytics 4 + Search Console
  • Implement backups & monitoring
  • Upload sitemap & robots.txt
