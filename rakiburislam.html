Rakibur Islam: A Visionary Entrepreneur

Bridging Technology, Healthcare & Employment,
Born on 15 October 2006 in Morigaon, Assam, India, Mr. Rakibur Islam is a young, dynamic, and forward-thinking entrepreneur who represents the new generation of Indian innovators. What truly sets him apart is the fact that he started his professional entrepreneurial journey at the age of just 15.

At an age when most students are still exploring basic career options, Rakibur Islam founded his software company, Future Top India, at only 15 years old.Today, at 19+ years of age, he stands as the Founder & Owner of multiple tech-driven initiatives, proving that vision and execution matter more than age.

Early Life & Educational Journey

Rakibur Islam completed his 12th standard from Laharighat Golden Senior Secondary School. From a very young age, he showed a strong inclination toward technology, systems, and innovation, which motivated him to step into the world of software development much earlier than his peers.

To strengthen his technical expertise, he pursued and successfully completed a Software Development Diploma in Delhi, gaining hands-on experience in development, real-world projects, and modern digital systems.

Currently, he is pursuing Bachelor of Pharmacy (B. Pharmacy) from Chaitanya Deemed to be University, Hyderabad, with completion expected in 2028. This rare combination of IT + Pharmacy allows him to work at the intersection of technology, healthcare, and artificial intelligence.

Entrepreneurial Beginning at 15

Rakibur Islam’s entrepreneurial mindset became reality at the age of 15, when he officially launched Future Top India as a software and technology services company. Starting at such a young age came with challenges—limited resources, experience gaps, and balancing education—but it also built exceptional discipline, confidence, and leadership skills.

Alongside his studies, he actively worked with small businesses, colleges, universities, and the medical field, understanding real operational problems and designing practical tech-based solutions.

Founder of Future Top India

  • Meet Panel & Technical Services

Rozgarfly reflects his belief that technology should solve real social problems, not just business needs.
